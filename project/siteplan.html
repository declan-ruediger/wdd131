Site Plan

Subject: Open-source Software Guides and Tips Content: My thoughts on open-source software, as well as practical tips and instructions for getting started. Reason: I have personal, hobby interest and passion about this kind of software, and have practical experience to share about it.

Site Name

"FOSS Ninja"

I chose FOSS Ninja because it sounds like a site where someone adept in Free and Open-Source Software would post their knowledge. It evokes imagery of skilled ninjas.

Site Purpose

This site serves as a blog for my experiences with FOSS Software, as well as a series of tutorials on how to set up different types of software.

Scenarios

  • How do I get started with FOSS software?
  • What are some common blunders that people make when starting out?
